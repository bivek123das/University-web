About Us

We are the World's largest University

ABC University is the largest university in the world, with more than 13,000 staff and 38,000 students from 150 countries. Founded in 1980 in the heart of London, ABC University was the first university in England to welcome students of any religion and the first to welcome women on equal terms with men.
